Choose Your Adventure on the Rapids

Trip 1- Morning Splash – Half-Day Adventure

Start your day with a splash on this fun, family-friendly ride through Crystal Current and Sunburst Run. Enjoy calm stretches between rapids, scenic canyon views, and plenty of laughter as our guides share stories about the river’s history. A perfect first taste of rafting excitement!

Trip 2- River Rush – Full-Day Expedition

This full-day adventure combines excitement and teamwork as you paddle through three of our most popular rapids. Conquer Thunder Falls before tackling Canyon Crusher, where teamwork and timing matter most. Enjoy a riverside lunch and time to swim in calmer sections along the way.

Trip 3- The Devil’s Challenge – Advanced Full-Day

Designed for thrill-seekers, this full-day challenge pushes your skills to the limit. Tackle the roaring Devil’s Drop, test your endurance on Thunder Falls, and finish strong through Canyon Crusher’s twisting finale. It’s the ultimate test of courage and teamwork.

Trip 4- Rapids & Campfire – Overnight Expedition

Adventure doesn’t end when the sun sets! Day one brings you through Crystal Current and Sunburst Run before camping riverside under a starlit sky. Day two takes on Thunder Falls and finishes with Canyon Crusher, combining thrill and beauty in one unforgettable trip.
