Utah

Utah, known as the “Beehive State,” is located in the Mountain West and is famous for its stunning natural landscapes, from the snow-capped Wasatch Mountains and the vast Great Salt Lake to the red rock canyons and arches of its five national parks. Utah blends cultural heritage with outdoor adventure, offering world-class skiing, hiking, and breathtaking desert scenery.
